WHO AM I?

Born in Seoul, raised in Montreal, and based in Toronto, I am a Product Designer with ample experience in financial services, insurance, and client/stakeholder management.

Inquisitive by nature, I specialize in human-focused design to solve real problems with simple and elegant solutions. I love asking questions and aim for perfection in my work, as I have years of client service experience.

I am obssessed with anything related to pineapples and I love to travel. I really love to use my imagination to come up with some creative designs and use my hands to bring them to life. For special events, I will make interactive cards and make delicious gift treats for my family and friends. Another interesting hobby of mine is making hand-crafted props to go with my costumes and occasionally you might see me wear my own hand-made outfits.
