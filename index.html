Enchantée!

Born in Seoul, raised in Montreal, and based in Toronto, I am a Product Designer with ample experience in financial services, insurance, and client/stakeholder management.

Inquisitive by nature, I specialize in human-focused design to solve real problems with simple and elegant solutions. I love asking questions and aim for perfection in my work, as I have years of client service experience.

My hobbies and interests include travel, gaming, and arts & crafts. I love to stretch my imagination and handicraft skills to create costumes, accessories, interactive cards and gifts - you name it! In my spare time, I like to go on websites such as CodePen and Dribbble to continue to inspire and challenge my own designs, or plug away at some coding challenges.
